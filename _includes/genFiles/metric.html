• time: Timestamp on the topology from which this metric was obtained. For headroom metrics this is the time at which the headroom plan was created.

  • entity_oid: Oid of the entity to which the metric applies.

  • type: The type of the metric.

  • provider_oid: Oid of the seller of this commodity.

  • key: The commodity key. This value can be null if no key is present.

  • current: Current utilization of the commodity on the selling entity

  • capacity: Capacity of the commodity on the selling entity

  • utilization: Utilization of the commodity on the selling entity.

  • consumed: Amount of the commodity currently used by the buying entity

  • peak_current: Peak amount of the commodity of the selling entity

  • peak_consumed: Peak amount of the commodity currently used by the buying entity

  • entity_type: The type of the entity in the entity_oid column.
